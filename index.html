What are AR Portals?

AR portals are doorways that open entrances to new and exciting virtual worlds. Using augmented reality, people can place virtual portals in real-world locations, which they can then walk through and be virtually transported into new dimensions.

How can I get involved?

Students can experience, share and receive AR portals as a part of joining our new app and program - UMD TerrARpin Getaway

Inside our Getaway app, everyone belongs!

All new students are placed into a micro-community with eventual friends and mentors who each share similar interest. Micro-communities include two mentors, four mentees, and the group can tailor their experience collectively which ever direction they desire.

Calling all experienced students!

Become a mentor to assist in shaping the future leaders of tomorrow, gain leadership skills and work with innovative technology.

Need help getting adjusted to campus life?

If you’re new to campus this is an opportunity to meet new people, learn techniques to be a successful college student and enjoy your interest with other like minded students.

Are you a commuter student?

No worries! The Getaway App is for all, if you don’t spend much time on campus or would describe yourself as introverted, you can still have fun and stay in the know with Campus AR Portals.
